Gold Nanoparticles

Fabrication of Gold Nanoparticles of Different Sizes and Shapes

Introduction

In this project, I independently designed the whole experiment, including the procedure of synthesizing gold nanoparticles (spheres and rods). So far, I have successfully synthesized gold nanoparticles with surface plasmon band ranging from 524nm to 1200nm, which can be further applied to enhance the fluorescent intensity of fluorescent reporter molecules.

 

Instructor: Wanwan Li

Investigator: Yulian Yao (Group Leader)

 

Project Description

Gold Nanoparticles

When the size of gold particles reduced to diameter range 1-10 nm (intermediate between the size of small molecules and that of bulk metal), they would display electronic structures, reflecting the electronic band structure of the nanoparticles, owing to quantum-mechanical rules. The Surface Plasmon Band (SPB) is one of the distinctive physical properties. The deep-red color of AuNP sols in water and glasses reflects the surface plasmon band, a broad absorption band in the visible region around 520 nm. By changing the shape of gold nanoparticles, the surface plasmon band can be adjusted in a wide range (520nm-1200nm). This project aims to synthesize gold nanorods with different aspect ratios in controlled weight, and further apply them to enhance the fluorescent intensity of fluorescent reporter molecules.
